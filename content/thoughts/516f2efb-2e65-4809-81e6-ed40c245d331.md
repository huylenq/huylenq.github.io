Don’t hoard notes just for the sake of it.

Note systems are helpful because they can deal with / mitigate the **forgetfulness nature** of our brain
- So you can program your future self.
- A way to revoke/unload the -past self into the current/future self
    - The quality and expressiveness of the note system affect how much information is lost during these unloading.
