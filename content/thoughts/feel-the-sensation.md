What we live for, no matter what the higher-order purpose we think of, is all about seeking and experiencing the sensation.

The emotional sensations, the core “good” feelings that you (up to a certain degree) can deliberately choose to pursue are the magnetic force that of <span class="private-link">My Compass</span>. For me, those are:

- The fulfillment of the "**this is it**" moment. When I enjoy life as it is. When the present is all I have, and it is enough.

- The feeling of **moving forward**, knowing that the mission is far away, even the direction, or what's at the end, is unknown; but I know I'm moving forward (usually more than I feel)

- Serenity - trust that everything is fine, I'm doing fine. A big part of this comes from [Life Running Digital Engine](/thoughts/life-running-digital-engine).
	- You can feel the imbalance happening. You by instinct, feel the urge to restore the balance, you like things balanced.
	- You feel the debts accumulated, and you like releasing them.
	- **Where there is expectation, there is debt**
