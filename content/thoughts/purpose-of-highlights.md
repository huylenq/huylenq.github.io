Why should I make highlights while reading? And what should be highlighted? How would they be used?

### The Types of Highlight

**Resonate**: the classic meaning of highlights. They are what connected me with the book, my owner personal feeling with them.
*Idea for resonate highlight: telescoped / roll-able context.*

**Asterisks**: to mark for ideas/concepts that need further delving for understanding.

### When to make  Highlights

Highlights should serve a purpose for **future action**, not to collect from the **fear of missing out**.
