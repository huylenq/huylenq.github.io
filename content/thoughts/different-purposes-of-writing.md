Countless times have you heard the advice: "You should write more". But write what? Writing is just an act, different "types" of writing have different **purposes**, different demanded and kind of **attention**, and different **measurements** of results.

I splits the act of writing into two different purposes, [Write to attend](/thoughts/write-to-attend) and [Write to understand and to transfer](/thoughts/write-to-understand-and-transfer).
