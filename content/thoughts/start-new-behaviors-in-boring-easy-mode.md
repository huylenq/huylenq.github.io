---
title: "When forming a new behavior, start in boring easy mode"
slug: "start-new-behaviors-in-boring-easy-mode"
---

 

It is easy to over enthusiastic, the initial hype causes you to underestimate the friction of adopting new habit once the hype is over.

Constraint yourself to an easy target: 10 minute of guitar practice, 10 flashcards, 1 note review per day. The itch is intentional, leave yourself hunger for the next day.
