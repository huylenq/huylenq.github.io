1. Develop your **system**.
2. Perfectionism is harmful, life is about moving forward.
3. Most precious lessons are in your every life
4. Your Ego is not you.
5. <span class="private-link">Don't stereotype yourself</span>.
6. Tolerate failures, leave rooms for others and yourself to learn.
7. You cannot calculate it all. Sometimes you just have to make decisions with little information at hands, and take responsibility for it.
8. Character is proven in wars, not in peace.
