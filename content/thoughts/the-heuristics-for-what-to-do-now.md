---
title: "The heuristics for what to do now"
slug: "the-heuristics-for-what-to-do-now"
---
 

Deep down you know what’s right to do. You just don’t usually listen. So, listen for:

- Things that you feel the **resistance**, that you always *avoided*
- Things that break your **balance**, put you in the *scarcity* mentality.
- Things that you **dream** of but don’t believe you can reach.

Those are *the North* of your compass
