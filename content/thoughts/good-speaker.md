What are the common traits in good speakers?

## His characters

He is down to earth.
He is humorous.
He is humble.
He is self-deprecating, that is equivalent to being humorous and humble at the same time.

## His speaking

He talks slowly.
He talks clearly.

## His looking

Yes, he is probably somewhat [good looking](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Halo_effect).
