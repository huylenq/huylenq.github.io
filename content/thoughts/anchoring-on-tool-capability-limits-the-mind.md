It doesn’t matter how good a tool is; the **bandwidth** of the brain is always the delimiting plateau.

Your mind is the **ultimate tool**. However, it can **decay** (needs repetition to maintain), and can be **inconsistent** (side effect of plasticity, it morphs every time, Hebb’s Law). But it is the true-est form of your thought as your mind.

Treating tool usage as experiments. Prepare to ditch or evolve out of it.

Boil it down to first principles, to the core functions. Then evaluate to your own personal workflows, what you are actually needed.
