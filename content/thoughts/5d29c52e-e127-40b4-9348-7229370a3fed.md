Knowledge is declarative.
Knowledge takes deliberate efforts to retrieve.

Skills are procedural.
Skills (once developed) are automated.
Skills require much more practice and repetition than knowledge.
Skills require and build up chunks.

### Skills practicing

- Organically, on the daily works.
- Deliberately, with fabricated problems.
	- Sources could be from GenAI or written on the internet.
- Contemplate
	- Build the mental model, linking to existing world understanding.
