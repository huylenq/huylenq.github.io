---
title: "Completeness learning bias"
slug: "completeness-learning-bias"
maturity: "seed"
---

Courses and books are path ways that converge to the same truths.
If you missed a lecture, a chapter, it doesn't mean a piece of knowledge is loss. But it means you will take another path the same truth eventually.
The right thing to do is focus to think hard to see what ideas, what truths hide behind the representation.
