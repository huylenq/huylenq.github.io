## The Mindset

Just do it, feel it, get relieved of trusting your system.
Remember that tangible results take time and patience.

## The Components

There are a couple of programmatic MindOS components:

- **Calendar** is the **process scheduler**.
- **To-do lists** are the **wait queues**.
- All the mental models: **Identities**, **Principles**, **Mental Models**, **Operation Modes/Contexts** are just implementation frameworks.

## Type of Processes

- **Programs**: They should be preemptive with timers.
- **Reflection**: A meta program at the start and end of a cycle. This is the source of MindOS plasticity.

## Qualities

- **Reliability**: how instructions are adhered to. In other words, discipline.
